• iPod Touch

    • The iPod Touch is a music player and handheld gaming device. It was marketed by Apple Inc. With a touchscreen-controlled user interface, it was also used as a digital camera, a web browser, and for messaging. It is very similar to the known iPhone, but it connects to the Internet only through Wi-Fi and does not use cellular network data, which is why it is not considered a smartphone. The iPod Touch was introduced in 2007 and by 2012, over 100 million units were sold. iPod touch models are sold by storage space and color.
