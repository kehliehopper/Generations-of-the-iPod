• iPod Classic

    • The iPod Classic was a portable media player, created and formerly marketed by Apple Inc. There were six generations of the Ipod Classic, as well as a spin-off that was later re-intergrated into the main iPod line. This Classic was available in silver or black from 2007 onwards, replacing the "signiture iPod white." In 2014, Apple discontinued the iPod Classic.
